Authentication is required to access this application. A valid access hash is needed to proceed.

How to access Append the access hash as a query parameter to the URL:
https://your-app-url/?hash=YOUR_ACCESS_HASH

Need help?

  • Open the app through its dashboard launch button.
  • Ask your administrator for the access hash.
  • Confirm the URL includes the correct ?hash= parameter.
  • Check that the hash hasn't been rotated since you last used it.
